How our
Cohort works ?

We have taken a different approach in our cohort sessions. Rather than directly jumping into course teachings. We conduct these various stages to determine the right course for you. Other stages are also mentioned below.

Stage 1

Psychometric Test

This is a cognitive assessment that we conduct to understand a candidate’s traits and abilities to thrive in a job or overall career.

Stage 2

Customized Course

Based on the responses from Stage 1, we prioritize top courses tailored to your traits and abilities and train you in it.

Stage 3

Grooming

We believe hard skills alone wont make you employable that's why we groom you in soft skills & mock interviews.

Stage 4

Final SATs

SkillArc Assessment Test is for evaluating what you learnt in cohort. The score of this test will be used by HRs for placements.
